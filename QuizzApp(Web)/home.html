Welcome to Quiz Timer App

Quiz Timer App is an interactive web-based platform designed to conduct online quizzes efficiently. It allows students to attempt quizzes within a fixed time while administrators manage questions easily.

About the Quiz Timer Application

The Quiz Timer App provides a smart environment for conducting online assessments with automatic timer functionality. The admin can add, update, or delete questions and set the exam duration. Students can attempt quizzes easily and receive instant results after submission. This system reduces manual effort and improves evaluation speed.

How This App Improves Learning Skills

Time Management

Students learn how to manage time effectively while solving questions under exam conditions.

Instant Feedback

Immediate results help students identify mistakes and improve performance.

Concept Clarity

Regular quiz practice strengthens understanding of important topics.

Self Assessment

Students can evaluate their preparation level through repeated testing.
